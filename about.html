Shears Barber Shop was founded with a passion for precision grooming and a commitment to creating a welcoming environment for all. Established in 2017, our mission is to blend timeless barbering techniques with modern styles to deliver exceptional results.

Our team of skilled barbers takes pride in every cut, shave, and style, ensuring each client leaves feeling confident and refreshed. We believe in the power of a great haircut to transform not just your look, but your entire day. Visit us and discover why Shears is more than just a barber shop—it's a community.

Shears is located on the Waterfront landing, between N.Columbus Drive and N.Michigan Aveune, right in the heart of Downtown.
