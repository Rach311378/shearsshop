Ezekiel Reed

With over 15 years of experience, Ezekiel specializes in precision fades and classic pompadour cuts. His attention to detail ensures every client leaves with a sharp, tailored look. Ezekiel is also renowned for his hot towel shaves, delivering a smooth, refreshing experience that harkens back to the golden age of barbering.

Josiah Torres

Josiah brings a modern flair to traditional barbering, excelling in textured crops and undercut designs. His steady hands and creative vision make him a favorite among clients seeking bold, contemporary styles. Josiah’s signature straight-razor shaves are a blend of technique and relaxation, leaving skin impeccably smooth.

Nathaniel is a master of versatility, crafting everything from buzz cuts to intricate line designs with ease. His passion for barbering shines through in his meticulous approach to every cut.
